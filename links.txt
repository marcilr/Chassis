Timelapsed build of a HD-Plex Fanless H1.SODD PC Case
April 1, 2015 - 7:00 pm
HDPLEX H1.SODD fanless computer case supports Thin ITX and Mini ITX
with 24PIN ATX plug on the right side.  When using mini ITX inside
HDPLEX H1.SODD, VLP (very low profile) memory is required otherwise
the memory will conflict with optical drive.  M.2/mSATA SSD is
required for H1.SODD.  Regular 2.5" SSD/HDD is NOT supported.
Own 2x 1TB Crucial M550 2.5 <=== Not M.2/mSATA 
http://www.hd-plex.com/blog/tag/hdplex-h1-sodd/
